This site is meant to be a portfolio of the work I do throughout my professional and un-professional career. I one day hope to be a full on web developer, and hopefully this site will display my ambitions and work I am proud of.


Background

I was born In Ottawa Ontario and have lived here for my entire life. As a child I was fascinated by music, art, and general creativity. This lead to me becoming interested in the Interactive Media Design at Algonquin because in reality web design and development is quite creative. There are so many different aspects to think about while designing a website, and working around these aspects to create something unique is something I find very engaging and fun.
